The Next Horizon

Challenges & Future

As physical AI integrates deeper into our infrastructure, we face new frontiers of technical and ethical challenges.

Operational Cyber Security

Shifting from data leaks to preventing physical misjudgments and unauthorized machine takeovers. Securing the physical layer is paramount.

Spatial Computing & Digital Twins

Simulating millions of environments in virtual worlds (e.g. NVIDIA Omniverse) before physical deployment to ensure absolute safety and efficiency.

Technical Bottlenecks

Solving the data scarcity issue in the physical world and overcoming the power/battery dilemma for heavy edge computing.
