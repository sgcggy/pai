The Evolution of Automation

1st Gen: Rule-based Automation

Repetitive, rigid coding without adaptation.

2nd Gen: Narrow AI & Robotics

Machine learning applied to specific, limited tasks.

3rd Gen: Physical AI Era

The ChatGPT Moment for the physical world. VLA (Vision-Language-Action) models enabling autonomous adaptation.
